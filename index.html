About Me

A scientist turned developer.

Originally, I began my career with a Bachelors in Geology working in the oil field of the Permian Basin (West Texas - for those non-Geology folks). Life took me in a different direction and I landed in an position working in IT in Eastern Iowa for John Deere where I developed (pun totally intended) a passion for programming. After a very intense program with General Assembly, I am now a full-stacked web-developer.

Along the way, I managed to turn two of my other passions, yoga and salsa-dancing, into side-hustles and I teach both weekly to beginners and intermediate/advanced students - although my preferred niche is beginners because I enjoy making people feel comfortable with a new skill.

I married into my final side-hustle and became co-owner of my husband's sound and lighting company, J&E Entertainment. On most weekends we can be found DJing weddings, graduation parties, birthday parties, you name it.
